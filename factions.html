Mordor

"Mordor is a realm surrounded by dark mountains, including orcs, black uruks, ologs and other dark creatures.
They are all ruled by the powerful Dark Lord, Sauron, and his servants, the Nazgûls.
While the Dark Lord lies in Barad-Dûr, the Witch-King takes the lead of the evil armies.
The Witch-King is so, the main leader of Mordor's faction and Minas Morgul became the new capital of Mordor.
He fought against Gondor with his allies, Gundamar, and took Pellenor Fields, Lossarnach, Dol Amroth, Lebennin and Ithilien.
Later, he gave a city of Lossarnach to Glupe, King of Bangad-Minhiriath, and made an economical alliance.
Mordor is the faction with the most of lands and claimbuilds. It is also one of the strongest faction, if it's not the strongest!" - Jukoz, Faction Leader of Mordor
