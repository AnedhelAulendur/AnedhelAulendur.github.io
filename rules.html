Explanations

Safe zone => A safe zone is a region claimed by your faction, or community places like spawn. Allied claim regions do not count as safe zones.
Plundering => Plundering is the looting of an Unbannered build. Griefing is not allowed in the process!
Claimbuild => A claim build is required to claim a region and has to be approved by staff and be bannered.
Region => When talking about regions, we always refer to the claim regions shown in #claim-regions.
